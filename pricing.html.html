Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our SMS services

How do SMS credits work?

One SMS credit is equivalent to one standard text message (160 characters) sent to one recipient. If your message exceeds 160 characters, it will use additional credits based on the length of the message.

Do the SMS credits expire?

No, your purchased SMS credits never expire. You can use them at your own pace without worrying about losing them.

How can I make a payment?

We accept payments via MTN Mobile Money and bank transfers through Zenith Bank. After selecting your plan, you will be guided through the payment process with detailed instructions.

Can I send messages to international numbers?

Yes, our service supports messaging to international numbers. However, sending messages internationally may consume more credits depending on the destination country.

How many contacts can I add to my account?

You can add up to 10,000 contacts to your account. These contacts can be organized into different groups for easier management and targeting.
